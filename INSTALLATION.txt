Installing Parrocchettami
=========================

This build supports Apple Silicon Macs and requires macOS 14 Sonoma or later.

Install
-------

1. Open the Parrocchettami DMG.
2. Drag Parrocchettami.app onto the Applications shortcut.
3. Open Applications in Finder.
4. Control-click Parrocchettami, choose Open, then confirm Open.

The Control-click step is necessary because this build is not notarized by Apple.

First launch
------------

Click Download Model when prompted. The one-time download is approximately
742 MB and needs roughly 1 GB of free disk space. After the download finishes,
all transcription runs locally and the app can be used offline.

The model is stored at:

~/Library/Application Support/Parrocchettami/models/

If macOS still blocks the app
-----------------------------

Only do this if you received the DMG directly from someone you trust. Open
Terminal and run:

xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine "/Applications/Parrocchettami.app"
open "/Applications/Parrocchettami.app"

Privacy
-------

Audio is processed locally. The app connects to Hugging Face only to download
the speech model during first-time setup.

Uninstall
---------

Delete the app from Applications. To also remove the downloaded model and local
transcription history, delete:

~/Library/Application Support/Parrocchettami/
